Graze | A healthy snack box

Let me start off by saying that I am a big time snacker. At work, I am always munching on various nuts, granola, chocolate, you name it. When I learned about Graze, I knew it was going to be a winner.

When you first subscribe to the snack service, Graze sends you a FREE sample box with a variety or four (4) snacks. Mine came with dried apples with toffee dipping sauce, rolled oat granola bars, sweet and spicy dried chickpea and beets trail mix, and a medley of dried cranberries, apples and rhubarb. As you can see, there was a wide spectrum of flavors and there wasn’t a bad snack in this first box. I even had to fight off my kids from some of the things I thought they would never in a million years eat (like dehydrated chickpeas and dried rhubarb).

After setting up my account on the easy to navigate Graze website, I was able to rank and review different types of snacks for future boxes based on flavors, nut and fruit types, and even allergies. I am a sucker for nuts and spice, so that’s what I specified when I decided to continue my subscription. Because my kindergarten daughter begged for more Graze treats, I surprised her with my next box which contained eight (8) snacks. Of course all were enjoyed.

If you are looking for a quick and easy variety of snack choices delivered to you weekly, I would not hesitate to suggest giving Graze a try. Each of their snacks are low calorie, low sugar and a good source of vitamins, protein and fiber. Pack them in your kids lunch boxes or take them to work!
