FabFitFun | Beauty, fitness & wellness

One of the most popular subscription boxes out there - even winning the #1 Best Subscription Box for Women in 2019. But is it just hype? Or is it really worth it? With the plethora of options and customizability, it’s worth trying to find the right box that works for you.

Each season they’ll send you a box of $200+ worth items in categories like beauty, fashion, and fitness. Every box has some common items that all subscribers receive ranging from said categories. But most of the box will include choice items. This past season included sunglasses, beach towels, gym bags, necklaces, palettes, and more!

All in all, I am happy I ordered, and I will stay a member for now. I'll decide whether or not to cancel after I get the Spring box, which I am looking forward to. I probably would have had more fun with this box if I'd let it be a surprise and refrained from watching any unboxing videos, but those are what got me to order in the first place. I do love trying new products (I literally have bins of cosmetics and beauty products) and I love that these are full-sized items. You can’t regift samples, and it’s a nice to have the bigger size if you end up falling in love with something.

I do wish they were a bit more transparent with the items you're getting, because I see a lot of people complaining about what DIDN'T come in their box. But my favorite part about FabFitFun is that now I have a little stash of items I can use as gifts, which is great for last minute invites or forgotten birthdays!
